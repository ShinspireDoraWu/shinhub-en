Harmonious Dialogue at Home & Work

#GourmetClub
Sep 30, 2024 Sat 11:30
Tasty Steak (Taipei Fuxing Branch)
Registration: Apr 1, 2024 - May 1, 2024

The Joint Meeting of the 7th Division invites Mr. Li Chong-jian to speak on "Harmonious Dialogue at Home and in the Workplace." The speaker shares common parent-child communication methods in life, noting that commands and lectures are often just habitual "I speak, you listen," rather than true dual-exchange dialogue. True dialogue starts with listening. The goal is not to make someone obey, but through mindful listening and sincere exchange, to explore potential difficulties, accept each other's perspectives, and eventually walk toward a shared goal together.
